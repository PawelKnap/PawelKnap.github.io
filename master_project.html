My master's project

In my master's dissertation, I am extending the research initiated during my summer research internship, once more working under the guidance of Dr. Hasung Kim. This phase involves leading a team of four students with the objective of improving the system's capabilities to enhance its practical applications. Our efforts include addressing challenges related to system range and pose estimation accuracy. Additionally, we are exploring the exclusive use of radar sensors for human pose estimation.
